# Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

Our standard MOQ guideline:

- **100 pcs per color / per specification**
- Mixed colors are acceptable within structured production planning
- Hook upgrades may require separate MOQ (depending on supplier policy)

For first-time cooperation, we recommend:

1) Review existing samples  
2) Confirm target market requirements  
3) Confirm packaging and hook options  
4) Proceed to sampling before bulk production  

For special hook brands (VMC / Mustad / BKK):

- MOQ depends on supplier quotation
- Lead time depends on hook manufacturer schedule
- Please contact our sales team for updated hook pricing and availability

---

# Lead Time

Lead time is calculated from:

**Final sample confirmation + official purchase order confirmation (email)**

Typical timeline:

- Sampling: 7–15 days
- Bulk production: 20–35 days (depending on quantity)
- Hook upgrade projects: may require extended lead time

For urgent projects, please communicate expected delivery timeline in advance.
