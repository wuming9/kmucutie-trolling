# Private Label Customization Scope

KMUCUTIE supports structured OEM customization for trolling lure brands.

---

## 1. Color Customization

- Custom color painting based on reference sample
- Pantone-based color confirmation
- Target fish application adjustment
- UV coating option

Before bulk production, a confirmed sample is retained for reference.

---

## 2. Packaging Customization

We support:

- Custom printed backing cards
- Blister packaging
- Hanging cards
- Logo printing
- Barcode printing
- Private label carton marking

Artwork files are required in AI / PDF format.

---

## 3. Hook Upgrade Options

We support hook replacement with:

- VMC
- Mustad
- BKK

Hook upgrade MOQ depends on:

- Hook supplier pricing
- Hook manufacturer production cycle

Please confirm hook requirements in advance to avoid lead time delay.

---

## 4. Other OEM Support

- Split ring upgrade
- Surface coating upgrade
- Saltwater corrosion resistance upgrade
- Packaging reinforcement for long-distance export
