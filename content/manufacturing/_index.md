# Manufacturing & Assembly

KMUCUTIE focuses exclusively on offshore trolling lure OEM production.

Our manufacturing approach combines skilled manual assembly with controlled batch production to ensure stable supply for distributors and private label brands.

---

## Skilled Assembly Team

Offshore trolling lures require experienced hands.

Our production team handles:

- Resin head casting and finishing  
- Hand painting and surface detailing  
- Skirt trimming and color matching  
- Crimped rigging assembly  
- Final manual inspection  

Each unit is assembled by trained technicians familiar with offshore trolling applications.

---

## Controlled Batch Production

Trolling lures are not mass industrial parts.  
They require batch-based production control to maintain consistency.

We operate under:

- Standardized head dimensions  
- Defined weight tolerance ranges  
- Controlled skirt color matching  
- Repeatable rigging specifications  

This ensures repeat orders maintain consistent structure and appearance.
