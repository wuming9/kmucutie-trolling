# Standard OEM Production Workflow

KMUCUTIE follows a structured B2B production model to ensure consistency.

---

## Step 1 – Sample Review

- Review existing samples or client reference samples
- Confirm structure and swimming action
- Confirm hook specification

---

## Step 2 – Sample Confirmation

- Produce customized sample
- Conduct swimming action test
- Saltwater corrosion resistance test (if required)
- Hook pull test
- Retain approved sample for reference

Bulk production will not start without written confirmation.

---

## Step 3 – Pre-Production Sample (PPS)

Before full-scale production:

- A mass-production sample is prepared
- Verified against approved reference
- Confirmed via photo or physical dispatch

---

## Step 4 – Mass Production

- Structured assembly process
- In-process quality checks
- Packaging preparation

---

## Step 5 – Final Inspection & Shipment

- Visual inspection
- Hook inspection
- Swimming balance verification (random sampling)
- Carton marking and export packing

Shipment arranged under agreed trade terms (EXW / FOB / etc.)
