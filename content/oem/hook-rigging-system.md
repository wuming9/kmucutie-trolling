## Offshore Hook & Rigging System

In offshore trolling lures, hook and rigging configuration directly determines structural durability and landing reliability.

At KMUCUTIE, hook systems are treated as controlled structural assemblies — not simple accessory attachments.

We configure hook and rigging systems specifically for offshore saltwater OEM production.

### Heavy-Duty Offshore Hook Upgrade

## Hook Size & Weight Balance

Hook size directly affects:

- Lure balance  
- Swimming angle  
- Drop speed  
- Strike efficiency  

Improper hook sizing can destabilize lure tracking.

OEM configuration ensures hook weight matches head structure and skirt length.

Hook selection is always integrated into full lure structural design.

## Rigging Configuration

Rigging alignment determines offshore reliability.

We control:

- Leader stiffness selection  
- Crimp positioning  
- Hook alignment angle  
- Loop symmetry  
- Load-bearing stress direction  

Improper rigging increases rotation risk and reduces hook-up rate.

Batch consistency is critical for offshore performance repeatability.
