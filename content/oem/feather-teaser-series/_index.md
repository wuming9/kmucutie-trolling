# Feather & Flasher Trolling Lure Series  
### Offshore Skirted Teaser Manufacturing

KMUCUTIE produces resin head feather trolling lures for offshore saltwater programs.  
These lures are commonly used as standalone trolling units or as teaser components within larger trolling systems.

This series supports OEM configuration and private label production.

---

## 1. Head Structure

Available head options:

- Resin head
- Bullet style
- Light-weight offshore teaser head

Size range example:

- 6.5 inch
- 7 inch
- 9 inch
- Custom lengths available

---

## 2. Skirt & Feather Options

Available materials:

- Feather flasher combination
- Nylon skirt
- PVC skirt
- Mixed layered skirt

Color patterns selectable from catalog.

---

## 3. Rigging Configuration

OEM clients may select:

- Unrigged
- Single hook rig
- Double hook rig
- Heavy-duty offshore hook upgrade

Leader diameter and crimp standard can be specified.

## 4. Application Scope

Used for:

- Offshore trolling programs
- Tuna and pelagic fishing supply
- System-level teaser integration
- Distributor product lines

- Skirt cutting and trimming
- Feather assembly line
- Rigging and crimp inspection
- Final QC before packing

Stable production supports repeat bulk orders.
