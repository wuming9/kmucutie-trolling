# Offshore Daisy Chain Rig System  
### Multi-Lure Trolling Assembly – OEM Production

The Daisy Chain System is a multi-lure trolling rig designed for offshore pelagic fishing.  
Unlike single trolling lures, this system integrates multiple teaser bodies into a structured drag pattern to increase visual attraction and trigger predatory strikes.

KMUCUTIE provides OEM production for complete daisy chain assemblies, including teaser configuration, leader system, hook setup, and private label packaging.

---

## 1. System Structure Overview

A standard daisy chain system includes:

- Lead teaser body (with or without hook)
- Multiple teaser squids or skirts
- Heavy-duty mono leader
- Crimped connection points
- Swivel and terminal rigging
- Optional bird or flasher head

All components are assembled and tested before shipment.

---

## 2. Teaser Body Options

Available teaser types:

- PVC squid
- Soft octopus body
- Feather teaser
- Resin head teaser
- Bird teaser (surface disturbance type)

Teaser length range:

- 4 inch
- 6 inch
- 8 inch
- Custom sizes available
