# OEM & Distributor Cooperation

KMUCUTIE focuses exclusively on offshore trolling lure manufacturing.  
We provide OEM production and private label supply for distributors, fishing brands, and importers.

Our goal is long-term B2B cooperation built on stable quality and consistent supply capability.

---

## 1. What We Manufacture

We specialize in:

- Resin head trolling lures  
- Bullet head heavy offshore lures  
- Acrylic head trolling lures  
- Cedar plug trolling series  
- Daisy chain systems  
- Spreader bar assemblies  
- Feather and teaser configurations  

We do not operate as a retail store.  
All production is structured for bulk supply and private label cooperation.

---

## 2. OEM Customization Scope

Clients may customize:

- Head structure and weight  
- Skirt material and pattern  
- Hook configuration (single / double / heavy duty)  
- Leader diameter and rigging strength  
- Color catalog selection  
- Private label packaging  

Samples can be produced after configuration confirmation.

---

## 3. Production & Supply Capability

- Batch production lines  
- Standardized assembly workflow  
- Manual quality inspection  
- Export-ready packaging  
- Flexible MOQ depending on configuration  

Lead time depends on product complexity and order volume.

## 4. Distributor Cooperation Model

We support:
